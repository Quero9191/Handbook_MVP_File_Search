## Purpose
Growth team drives awareness, user acquisition, and retention through data-driven campaigns and optimization.

## Scope
- Applies to: Marketing campaigns, analytics, and user engagement
- Doesn't apply to: Product roadmap (see product/ instead)

## Growth Pillars

### Acquisition
- Run campaigns (email, paid ads, social)
- Measure CAC and conversion rates
- Optimize landing pages

### Retention
- Monitor user engagement metrics
- Run retention campaigns
- Gather customer feedback

### Analytics
- Track campaigns with UTM parameters
- Build dashboards
- Share insights with leadership
